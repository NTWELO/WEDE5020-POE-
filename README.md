WEBSITE PROPOSAL FOR NTWELLOW BLOOMS 

1.	Organisation overview 
Name: Ntwellow Blooms
History: Ntwellow Blooms was founded in 2022, right during Covid-19 pandemic. It emerged as a symbol of resilience and renewal. We are a bespoke florist based in Nelspruit. The organisation started with a vision to bring vibrancy, creativity, and community connection through floral artistry and sustainable design (Kotler & Keller, 2016).
 

Figure 1: Andy Lee, Bouquet. (pexels.com,2026)

Mission:
 To inspire joy and healing through innovative floral creations while promoting sustainability and community engagement (Chaffey, 2020).

Vision: 
To become a leading floral and lifestyle organisation that represents resilience, beauty, and growth in South Africa and beyond.



2. Target Audience

The organisation’s target audience includes individuals looking for floral arrangements for personal or corporate events, event planners, corporate clients, eco-conscious consumers, and local community members. Looking and segmenting audiences is vital for effective digital marketing strategies (Chaffey, 2020). Systems analysis principles display the importance of understanding user needs before designing solutions (Satzinger, Jackson & Burd, 2016).

3. Website Goals and Objectives

• Teach customers about sustainability and the organisation’s values.
• Build trust and engagement through customer reviews.
• Increase sales and grow market reach beyond local communities.
• create an extraordinarily strong online presence and brand identity.
• Display products, services, and portfolio.
• Provide an easy-to-use e-commerce platform for online ordering flowers and décor.



4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality
below is the proposed website features and functionality that align with best practices in e-commerce development (Shopify, 2023);
• Homepage: Engaging visuals, mission statement, and featured products.
• About Us: Organisation history, mission, vision, and team profiles.
• Product: High-quality images, descriptions, and pricing.
• Contact Page: Inquiry form, location map, and contact details.
• Reviews: Highlight positive experiences.
• Social Media Integration: Links and feeds from Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.
• Newsletter Signup: Email marketing integration.



5. Design and User Experience

• Clean, modern, and nature-inspired design with vibrant floral imagery.
• Mobile-first responsive layout.
• Intuitive navigation with clear categories.
• Accessibility features (alt text, readable fonts, contrast compliance).
• Emotional storytelling through visuals and copywriting.



6. Technical Requirements

The website will be built using HTML, with reliable hosting and SSL certification. Payment gateways such as PayjustNow, Ozow or Eft will be integrated. SEO optimization and analytics will be created to improve visibility (Shopify, 2023).

7. Timeline and Milestones

• Week 1–2: Discovery and planning (requirements gathering, wireframes).
• Week 3–4: Design mock-ups and approval.
• Week 5–6: Development (CMS setup, e-commerce integration).
• Week 7: Content population (text, images, product listings).
• Week 8: Testing (functionality, responsiveness, security).
• Week 9: Launch and training.
• Week 10: Post-launch support and optimization.




8. Budget (Estimated)

• Design & Development: R50,000 – R60,000
• Hosting & Domain: R4,000 – R7,000 annually
• Maintenance & Updates: R3,500 – R6,000 monthly
• Marketing: R11,000 – R16,000 initial setup



This proposal provides a professional framework that balances storytelling, technical detail, and business objectives.
